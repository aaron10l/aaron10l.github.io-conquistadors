WHAT WE DO

Our goal is to help as many kids as we can with STEM (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics).

How do we do this? Our organization partners with local Latino churches in the greater Cincinnati area to host weekly study sessions where we provide free hands-on help with our students' homework.

HOW TO JOIN

We host weekly study sessions at the Iglesia Adventista del Septimo Dia Church in West Price Hill!

Join our WhatsApp groupchat to learn more!
